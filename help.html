Trading Rules

Every day, the system automatically generates and distributes commodity orders to the user. Users can complete the payment of each order through USDT to obtain 0.6% commission. Large commission orders are randomly distributed. USDT can be withdrawn after orders. (If the orders are not completed, the system will automatically stop until the surplus order amount of the day) Each account can only be bound to a USDT account. If an error occurs, please contact customer service.

How to recharge

Before recharge, please bind your withdrawal address (support TRC-20) USDT address. You can click the recharge on the APP or webpage, and select the blockchain (TRC-20) you use for recharge.

How to withdraw money

After completing the daily order delivery, how to withdraw it normally, and get the account within 24 hours after starting the withdrawal. Wait for system verification to pass, and there is no withdrawal restriction.
